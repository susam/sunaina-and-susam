Our Story

We met each other at RSA. As we got to know each other we found that we have many shared interests since childhood, from collecting magnets and love for water bodies to listening to Coldplay and travelling. As time went by, we graduated from talking about programming and puzzles to watching movies, visiting museums and a number of hot spots in Bangalore.

One day, one of us (the guy of course) proposed the other across a coffee table. The girl thought a lot and asked a lot of questions but didn't say anything that would make the guy's day. Like all popular love stories, the girl took her time. The guy kept trying. He wrote a song for her.

MP3 | Score

She listened to it and smiled. A few months passed. One day, she said those three magic words back to the guy. We have been together since then.

Together we experienced our first snow. Together we have learnt to spot the planets in the night sky. Together we have pondered upon the mysteries of the universe we live in. Together we have grown to be better persons. Together we have promised to be true to each other in good times and in bad, in sickness and in health, and love and honour each other all the days of our lives.
